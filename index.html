Cai Shaofeng

I am a Senior Research Fellow in the Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore.
I mainly work on adaptive neural networks, focusing on adaptive neural architectures and deep learning techniques .
My research extends to anomaly detection, tabular deep learning, and in-database analytics, advised by Prof. Ooi Beng Chin.
